Top Blockbusters of 2024

Blockbusters Awards Upcoming News References

Inside Out 2

$1,698,217,953

The sequel to Pixar's acclaimed "Inside Out" explores Riley's teenage years with new emotions like anxiety and hope. Directed by Pete Docter, it combines heartfelt storytelling with cutting-edge animation.

Fans praised its insightful portrayal of adolescence and emotional growth, making it a box office success.

Deadpool & Wolverine

$1,337,900,827

Marvel's highly anticipated crossover blends Deadpool's humor with Wolverine's grit, directed by Shawn Levy. The film faced challenges in balancing tones but succeeded in delivering a cohesive narrative.

Fans lauded Ryan Reynolds and Hugh Jackman's chemistry, leading to strong box office performance.

Despicable Me 4

$968,240,710

Gru and the Minions embark on a new adventure involving a mysterious artifact. Directed by Chris Renaud, the film introduced fresh settings and characters while retaining its signature humor and heart.

Audiences praised its family-friendly appeal, contributing to substantial box office earnings.

Dune: Part Two

$714,444,358

Denis Villeneuve's epic continuation of Frank Herbert's saga delves deeper into Paul Atreides' journey on Arrakis. The production featured expansive desert landscapes and intricate set designs, bringing the complex world of Dune to life.

The film was praised for its visual grandeur and faithful adaptation of the source material. Fans and critics alike commended its storytelling and performances, leading to impressive box office numbers.

Godzilla x Kong: The New Empire

$571,750,016

This blockbuster pits two legendary titans against a new threat. Directed by Adam Wingard, the film showcases massive battles and state-of-the-art visual effects. The production involved extensive CGI work to create the colossal creatures and their epic confrontations.

Fans of the MonsterVerse celebrated the film's action sequences and spectacle. Its global appeal contributed to strong box office performance.

Kung Fu Panda 4

$547,634,675

Po returns in this animated adventure, facing new challenges and discovering hidden truths about his past. Directed by Jennifer Yuh Nelson, the film combines martial arts action with heartfelt storytelling. The production continued DreamWorks' tradition of high-quality animation and engaging narratives.

Audiences enjoyed the film's humor and action, praising its animation quality. The franchise's enduring popularity was evident in its box office success.

Beetlejuice Beetlejuice

$451,072,781

This sequel to the cult classic brings back the mischievous ghost for more supernatural antics. Directed by Tim Burton, the film blends gothic aesthetics with dark humor. The production reunited much of the original cast, adding to its nostalgic appeal.

Fans appreciated the return of beloved characters and the film's unique style. Its mix of comedy and horror elements resonated with audiences, leading to solid box office returns.

Venom: The Last Dance

$439,935,445

The latest entry in the Venom series explores Eddie Brock's complex relationship with the symbiote. Directed by Andy Serkis, the film features intense action sequences and character development. The production aimed to delve deeper into the duality of its protagonist.

The film received mixed reviews but was praised for its action and Tom Hardy's performance. Its established fan base contributed to its box office success.

Bad Boys: Ride or Die

$404,516,167

Detectives Mike Lowrey and Marcus Burnett return for another high-octane adventure. Directed by Adil & Bilall, the film combines action and humor, staying true to the franchise's roots. The production featured elaborate set pieces and stunts.

Fans enjoyed the chemistry between the leads and the film's entertainment value. Its blend of action and comedy appealed to a broad audience, resulting in strong box office numbers.

Kingdom of the Planet of the Apes

$397,378,150

This installment explores the aftermath of Caesar's legacy and the rise of a new ape leader. Directed by Wes Ball, the film combines thought-provoking themes with visual spectacle. The production utilized advanced motion capture technology to bring its characters to life.

The film was praised for its storytelling and visual effects. Its exploration of complex themes resonated with audiences, contributing to its box office performance.
